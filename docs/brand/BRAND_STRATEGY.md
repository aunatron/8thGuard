# Brand Strategy

8thGuard is a premium global crypto safety brand.

Positioning:
- global-first
- Ghana/Africa as launch wedge, not limitation
- paid-first utility
- wallet intelligence and transaction safety before funds move
- future guarded transaction readiness

Core line: "Check before you send."

Support lines:
- "Crypto has speed. Now it needs a guard."
- "Every transaction has a before and after. 8thGuard lives in the before."
- "Scammers want urgency. 8thGuard gives you pause."
- "Your wallet has no undo button. Check first."
- "Fraud moves fast. Protection must move faster."

Proof language must stay qualified: early risk signals, risk indicators, wallet intelligence, MVP result, and not final fraud proof.
