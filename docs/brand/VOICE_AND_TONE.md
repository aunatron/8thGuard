# Voice And Tone

8thGuard should sound bold, premium, urgent, and credible.

Use:
- "Free education. Paid utility."
- "Checks and reviews are paid services."
- "Early risk signals, not final fraud proof."
- "Official payment rails only."
- "No private keys. No seed phrases."

Avoid:
- fake scarcity
- fake testimonials
- guaranteed safety claims
- final fraud-proof claims
- language that suggests live production escrow, custody, exchange, trading, or recovery guarantees
