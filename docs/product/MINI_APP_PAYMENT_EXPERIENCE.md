# Mini App Payment Experience

The product is a Telegram bot today and should be ready for mini app and web app payment flows.

Mini app goals:
- show paid catalog
- collect wallet, transaction, agent, or guarded-send inputs
- create payment session
- show Stripe/Polar, Paystack/Others, and official crypto rails
- accept structured payment proof
- unlock entitlement after confirmation
- display early risk signals and manual review status

The mini app must keep the same safety boundaries as the bot: no private keys, no seed phrases, no exchange, no custody, and no production escrow.
