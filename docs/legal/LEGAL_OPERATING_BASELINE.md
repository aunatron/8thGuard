# Legal Operating Baseline

This is an operational policy pack for 8thGuard. It is not a substitute for counsel review.

## Business-protective positions
- 8thGuard provides risk indicators and review services, not final fraud proof.
- 8thGuard does not provide escrow, custody, exchange, trading, brokerage, money transmission, or user-to-user settlement.
- 8thGuard does not request seed phrases, private keys, wallet login credentials, or unnecessary identity documents in Telegram.
- Service payments are for digital review services only.
- Wrong-network crypto payments may be unrecoverable.
- Manual reviews can be declined if the request is abusive, unlawful, incomplete, or outside scope.

## Privacy posture
- Minimize data collected through Telegram.
- Do not sell raw private user data.
- Keep audit logs and service records only as long as needed for service, security, compliance, and abuse prevention.
- Give users a clear path to contact 8thGuard about data requests.

## Global-to-local positioning
8thGuard should present itself as a global crypto safety platform while showing practical awareness of Ghanaian and African P2P realities: Mobile Money, local rails, Telegram/WhatsApp groups, informal agents, and high-pressure payment requests.
