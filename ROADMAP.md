# Roadmap

## Phase 0 (Now)
- Telegram bot webhook MVP on Vercel.
- Real Wallet Check v0 with limited live public blockchain signals.
- Payment readiness for Paystack service links and official crypto wallet instructions.
- Structured audit logs.

## Phase 1
- Supabase-backed audit/event store.
- Payment submissions and invoice/reference tracking.
- Agent verification registry.
- Case management for scam reports.

## Phase 2
- Broader multi-chain transaction intelligence providers.
- Reputation graph and fraud signatures.
- Escrow-readiness architecture (without custody).
