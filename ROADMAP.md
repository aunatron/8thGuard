# Roadmap

## Phase 0: Premium MVP
- Premium Telegram bot with inline menus and paid-first service catalog.
- Paid website and `/pay` page with Paystack link readiness.
- Official crypto wallet payment instructions for service payments only.
- Real Wallet Check v0/v1 with limited live public-chain signals and graceful fallbacks.
- Structured audit logs and Supabase-ready data model.

## Phase 1: Paid Operations
- Payment proof intake, manual review queue, invoices, and entitlement unlock.
- Supabase-backed audit logs, risk checks, scam reports, and ledger entries.
- Agent verification review workflow and community safety review process.
- Mini-app payment experience with session IDs and safer handoff.

## Phase 2: Multichain Intelligence
- Broader EVM, TRON, TON, Solana, BTC, and XRP adapters.
- Transaction intelligence provider expansion.
- Risk model versioning and source confidence scoring.
- Group/community reputation graph with qualified risk indicators.

## Phase 3: Guarded Transaction Readiness
- Chain-agnostic payment session engine.
- Fee quote engine for checks, reviews, guarded sessions, and future protected flows.
- Smart contract, multisig, PSBT, and chain-specific protection research.
- Legal, security, and compliance gates before any custody-like or escrow-like production behavior.
