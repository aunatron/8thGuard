# Quick Check vs Paid Report

## Quick Check (Preview)

Quick checks are short, branded previews returned by `/check_wallet`, `/check_tx`, and `/check_agent`.

Quick checks include:
- Detected network
- Simple risk score (score/100 and level)
- Maximum 3 signals
- Compact CTA to /pricing or /pay
- Short disclaimer

Quick checks do NOT include:
- Full source breakdown
- Detailed methodology
- Multiple explorer links
- Transaction pattern analysis
- Community report cross-references
- Manual review notes
- Shareable safety summaries

## Paid Report (Full Intelligence)

Paid reports are structured premium results delivered after a paid entitlement is confirmed.

Paid reports include:
- Deeper risk signals with context
- Source breakdown (which APIs, data feeds, and public chains were queried)
- Explorer evidence links (multiple networks)
- Transaction pattern review
- Community report lookup and cross-reference
- Manual notes where applicable
- Payment/session reference
- Shareable safety summary
- Timestamp and branded report header

## Group Mode

When a quick check is used in a Telegram group, the response is even shorter:
- No signal details
- No explorer links
- Risk level and score only
- CTA to DM the bot for full report

Group responses act as clean public teasers that drive DM traffic.

## Pricing Ladder

- Quick checks are paid preview checks (entry tier).
- Detailed reports provide deeper wallet, transaction, or agent intelligence.
- Manual reviews are higher-value products with human annotation.
- Priority and enterprise tiers include follow-up, documentation, and multi-check access.

## Rule

Quick Check = preview.
Paid Report = full intelligence.

Free education. Paid utility. Early risk signals, not final fraud proof.
