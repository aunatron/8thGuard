# Chain-Agnostic Escrow Strategy

This document is readiness planning only. 8thGuard does not provide production escrow or custody in the MVP.

Possible future patterns:
- EVM smart contracts after audit and legal review.
- Bitcoin multisig or PSBT workflows after custody analysis.
- XRP destination tag and account safety checks.
- TON, Solana, and TRON chain-specific protection systems after provider review.

Gates before launch:
- legal counsel review
- security architecture review
- audit logging and dispute workflow
- compliance posture
- user disclosure and consent
- incident response runbook
