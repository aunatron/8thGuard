# Fee Engine Architecture

The fee engine quotes 8thGuard service fees separately from network/gas fees.

Current fixed service references:
- Quick checks start at $4.99.
- Detailed reviews start at $9.99.
- Premium reviews range from $14.99 to $199.99.

Future guarded payment sessions may charge a fixed fee or percentage. Future protected transactions may charge a service/protection fee. Network and gas fees are paid to networks, not 8thGuard.
