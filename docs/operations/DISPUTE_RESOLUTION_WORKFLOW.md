# Dispute Resolution Workflow

Dispute resolution is future readiness for guarded flows and premium review operations.

MVP dispute intake:
- collect product selected
- collect Stripe/Polar order reference, Paystack reference, or crypto transaction hash
- collect wallet, transaction, agent, or session context
- collect Telegram contact
- collect short timeline

Do not collect seed phrases, private keys, wallet passwords, or unnecessary identity documents in Telegram.

Future guarded-flow disputes require persistent audit logs, session IDs, invoices, ledger entries, and clear status history.
