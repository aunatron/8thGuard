# Counsel Review Checklist

Before heavy commercial scale, ask qualified counsel to review:

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Risk Disclaimer
- Payment Policy
- Refund Policy
- Ghana Data Protection Commission registration obligations
- Cross-border data transfer posture
- Stripe/Polar and Paystack/Others payment terms and refund handling
- Crypto payment wording and money-service risk
- Consumer protection and unfair-contract issues
- Defamation risk around reports about agents, groups, or wallets
- Law-enforcement request handling
- Data retention and deletion processes

Keep product language conservative: risk indicators, not final fraud proof.
