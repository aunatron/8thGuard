# Business Model

8thGuard is paid-first: free education, paid utility.

- Quick wallet, transaction, and agent checks start at $4.99.
- Detailed reviews start at $9.99.
- Premium review paths cover scam reports, agent verification review, group/community safety, and founding partner support.
- Stripe/Polar and Paystack/Others are for 8thGuard digital service payments only.
- Official crypto wallets are for 8thGuard digital service payments only.
- Future B2B/API work can serve communities, platforms, and risk teams without making 8thGuard an exchange, broker, custodian, or trading desk.

Every offer must keep the promise qualified: early risk signals, wallet intelligence, risk indicators, and MVP results, not final fraud proof.
